Welding Processes We Use In Our Shop

Stick Welding (SMAW)

The most heavy duty process we offer. Great for full penetration, open root, welding which is as strong as it gets. This is also the most commonly used process when we are out in the fields welding for customers.

Mig Welding (GMAW)

This process is very strong, but more importantly very fast. Used in a any application it puts down an extrodinary amount of metal in a short span of time. It is also great for projects requiring thinner steel.

Tig Welding (GTAW)

If your project is made of aluminium, chances are we are going to Tig weld it. AC Tig welding is the most common way to weld aluminum in our shop. Tig is also the most artistic welding process there is, so the end results are breathtaking and strong.

CNC Plasma Cutting

Custom CNC plasma cutting is another service we offer to customers, whether a customer has a part they need 100 of, or a custom part for a project, CNC plasma cutting is the way to go. Currently, we have a 4' x 8' table, run by a Hyperthem Powermax 1000 plasma cutter. A great combination to handle all of your cutting needs
